# Future Features — Roaming Records

## Core Functionality
- **Trip detail page** — click a trip to see a full view with photos, notes, ratings, dates
- **Add/edit trip form** — actually log a new trip with a modal or dedicated page
- **Wishlist page** — separate from My Trips, a list of places you want to go

## Engagement
- **Search & filter** — filter My Trips by country, continent, year, or rating
- **Trip stats dashboard** — total countries visited, miles traveled, favorite continent
- **Map view** — a world map with pins on visited/wishlisted destinations (Leaflet.js or react-simple-maps)

## Social / Profile
- **User profile page** — avatar, bio, home country, travel stats
- **Trip sharing** — generate a shareable link for a specific trip

## UI / Polish
- **Animated page transitions** — smooth fade/slide between routes
- **"On this day" section** — surface a past trip logged on today's date

## Content
- **Weather widget** on destination cards (free weather API)
- **Currency converter** — useful context when viewing a destination
